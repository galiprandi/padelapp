## 2025-05-15 - [Accessibility & Tactile Polish]
**Learning:** Adding screen-reader-only labels (sr-only) for visual-only cues (like red asterisks) significantly improves accessibility for mandatory fields without altering the visual design. Icons should always be aria-hidden if they are decorative to reduce screen reader noise.
**Action:** Always include sr-only context for visual indicators and aria-hidden for decorative icons in future UI refinements.
